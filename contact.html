David Jones is a British photographer born in St.Albans, England. His interest in photography was spurred by the works of photojournalists and candid photographers of the mid-twentieth century. Rambling far and wide across the endless city he lets the encounters amongst its inhabitants be the guide for his unobtrusive snaps; an emotional imprint of the everyday, whether it is imbued with a touch of the comic or the scuff of disagreement.

David has self-published a handful of books comprised of photographs taken in a variety of cities and countries. He also initiated and ran an employee gallery for two years.

As an adjunct to his candid photographs, David has an ongoing series of works dubbed 'The Follies'. Spurning post hoc image editing he instead uses bric-a-brac and physical effects, such as shooting through various materials, to produce images of an undefinable charm.

David currently lives and works in Toronto, Canada.
