Class RepairEngineer

RepairEngineers are invoked at the top of server loops and the like in order to perform damage control after such events as a conservative GC or a conservative purge in response to an resource emergency with a deep stack. These modules should implement subclasses of RepairEngineer (RE) which implement the method repair.
REs are registered by construction and deregistered by destruction.
